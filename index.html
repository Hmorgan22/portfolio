Pig Dice Game

This project is the "Pig Dice Game" that was created in 1945. The user will click the roll dice button to simulate the roll of 6-sided die. The number that the die rolls will be added into the player's current score field. The player can continue rolling the die until they either roll a 6, which will cause them to lose all of their current points, or until the decide they want to hold. Holding will convert the players current score into their total score.The first player to reach 100 points in their total wins.

Languages used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.

Number Guessing Game

This project is a number guessing game. The game will randomly generate a number between 1 and 20 when the game starts. The player will then have a score of 20 points and each incorrect guess of the number will subtract 1 point. This game will also tell the player if their guess is too high or too low depending on the secret number and the players guess. Once the player guesses the correct number the screen will change colors and check if the player's score is a new highscore.

Languages used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
